Mats Valk

Mats Valk is a Dutch speedcuber and one of the most successful cubers in history. He has won numerous competitions and held multiple world records in various events, including the 3x3 single solve and the 4x4 single solve. In 2015, Valk approached the Chinese company QiYi MoFangGe with an idea for a new cube design that would incorporate several key features to improve speed and performance. He worked closely with the company's designers to create the Valk 3 cube, which was released in 2016. The cube quickly became a favorite among speedcubers worldwide, with many citing its smooth, fast turning and innovative design as major factors in their success. The Valk 3 has since undergone several updates and iterations, with Valk and QiYi MoFangGe continuing to work together to push the boundaries of cubing technology and innovation.
